# Feature 009: Weekly Founder Brief

## Overview
Implements Feature 009 (Spec 009). A proactive summary artifact generated every Monday (or on demand) that answers: "What is the health of my product strategy?"

## Logic
1. **Aggregation**: Pulls all active bets and their recent interventions.
2. **Synthesis**:
    - "Improving": Bets with resolved risks.
    - "At Risk": Bets with unaddressed risks or low conviction.
    - "Weekly Question": A provocative prompt generated by the Brief Builder.
3. **UI**: `BriefCard` rendered as an artifact in the chat.
